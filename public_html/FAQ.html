Your Questions

What is BentoGo?

BentoGo was created from a love of food and all things cute. We sought out to create the most convenient yet beautiful bentos in the market. We've taken each of your favourite characters from classic cartoons, anime, and general media, and made them into little parcel of deliciousness. Each bento is made with a great deal of love, and designed to delight the hearts of all.

Where are you located?

We have a number of locations throughout the Greater Vancouver Area. Our main store is located near Surrey Central, however feel free to click here to find the closest location to you!

How are your bentos made?

We believe in using the most sustainable and organic ingredients. We get all our product and items from local suppliers and farms. Our team then prepares each box hand-made to ensure that your box is specially designed for your needs.

What is your shipping policy?

We have a flat rate of $2.50 because we understand the pains of paying for shipping, but we still need to feed our delivery men.

How do custom orders work?

The minute your customized order is processed, we begin to build it from scratch so that you can get your perfect bento fresh and hot! Our team then hand-prepares each individual box to ensure that your box is specially designed for your needs.

How are you so amazing?

It's just a natural thing we were born with.

Can I steal your company?

We would greatly appreciate if you did not steal our company. However, you're more than welcome to send your any applications to apply to careers@bentogo.com. We're always looking for great people to join our team.
