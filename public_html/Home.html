Why we matter

Made to Order

We make all of our bentos the minute we get your order so we can guarantee that when you receive your bento, the food is still warm and ready to eat. What else could be better than getting a warm meal made with love?

Quality Ingredients

We offer a wide variety of ingredients at BentoGo - from fresh fish to beef teriyaki, we've got you covered. And don't worry, if you're vegetarian, there's plenty of options for you too.

Good for you and your city

Not only is our food delicious, but it is sustainable as well. We try our best to only purchase our ingredients from local sources, supporting your city's farms and economy!
